# Forward Deployed Engineer Is AI's Hottest Job as OpenAI and Google Race to Hire

## Summary
The role of "Forward Deployed Engineer" (FDE) has emerged as one of the most sought-after positions in the AI industry, with OpenAI and Google aggressively recruiting for these roles. This article explores what FDEs do and how to break into this field.

## Key Points
- FDE roles are in high demand at leading AI companies (OpenAI, Google)
- FDEs bridge the gap between AI research and real-world applications
- Requires strong engineering skills plus domain expertise
- Growing career path in the AI infrastructure and deployment space

## Steps
1. Build strong foundation in ML/AI systems engineering
2. Gain experience deploying AI models in production environments
3. Develop domain expertise in target industry (healthcare, finance, etc.)
4. Contribute to open-source AI projects and build a portfolio
